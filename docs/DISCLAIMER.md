Mu Trading Bot is a software engineering portfolio project.

It is not financial advice, investment advice, or a recommendation to buy or sell any asset. It does not guarantee profit and it does not predict market outcomes.

The system does not execute trades automatically. It only creates alerts, records decisions, and stores basic backtesting results. The user is responsible for their own investment decisions and for verifying market data, liquidity, risk, and execution conditions.

Past performance does not guarantee future results. Backtesting results are simplified and may not reflect real trading outcomes.
